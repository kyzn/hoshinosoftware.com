Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does the app do?
    It fills your screen with the current local time: hours, minutes, and seconds. The digits stack vertically in portrait and sit on one line in landscape. That's it, by design: no settings, no setup.

  • Does the screen stay awake?
    Yes. While the clock is showing, your device will not auto-lock, so it works as a bedside, desk, or stage clock. Lock the device or leave the app to let the screen sleep again.

  • Will it drain my battery?
    Keeping any screen on uses battery, but the app is designed to minimize it: the display is pure black with white digits, which uses very little power on OLED screens. For all-night use, we recommend plugging the device in.

  • Is the time accurate?
    The app shows your device's clock, which iOS keeps synchronized automatically. If the time looks wrong, check Settings → General → Date & Time → Set Automatically.

  • Can I switch to a 12-hour format?
    Not currently. The app always shows 24-hour time (HH:MM:SS), matching its name.

  • Does it need an internet connection?
    No. HH:MM:SS works completely offline.

  • Does it collect my data?
    No. The app makes no network requests and stores no personal data. Nothing leaves your device.
